Game Levels & Challenges

Explore a variety of worlds filled with challenging levels! Dash Master!

  • Cityscape Dash – Run through neon-lit skyscrapers.

    • The player controls a character who runs on the rooftops of skyscrapers illuminated by neon lights.
    • The goal is to cover as much distance as possible, avoiding obstacles and collecting power-ups.
    • The game is characterized by a fast pace, bright graphics and dynamic music.

  • Jungle Escape – Avoid vines and quicksand in the deep forest.

    "Jungle Escape" is a game concept that immerses the player in an intense adventure in a dense tropical jungle.

    Here is a detailed description:
    The essence of the game:
    • The player finds himself in the heart of the wild jungle, where he has to fight for survival.
    • The goal is to overcome dangerous obstacles such as vines, loose sands, wild animals and other dangers to get out of the jungle.
    • The game combines elements of action, adventure and survival.

  • Space Rush – Navigate zero-gravity zones with rocket boosts.

    "Space Rush" – this is a game concept that combines fast-paced racing with elements of weightlessness and the use of rocket boosters.

    Here is a detailed description:
    • The player controls a spacecraft that rushes through difficult tracks in zero gravity.
    • The goal is to cover the track as quickly as possible, using rocket boosters to maneuver and accelerate.
    • The game is characterized by high speed

  • Lava Fortress – Survive fire traps and molten obstacles.

    "Lava Fortress" is a game concept that combines elements of action, survival and platforming in a dangerous fire environment.

    Here is a detailed description:
    The essence of the game:
    • The player finds himself in an ancient fortress located in the heart of the volcano.
    • The goal is to overcome dangerous fire traps, molten obstacles, and enemies to get out of the fortress.
    • The game is characterized by a high level of difficulty, an atmospheric fire environment.
